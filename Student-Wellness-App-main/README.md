# Student-Wellness-app
A Streamlit app for analyzing student journal entries and tracking wellness

# 🌿 NatureMind Wellness

[![Streamlit App](https://static.streamlit.io/badges/streamlit_badge_svg)](https://naturemind-wellness.streamlit.app/)
[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)

**NatureMind** is an authentic, nature-inspired mental wellness companion built with Streamlit. It bridges the gap between digital tracking and natural living by analyzing student journal entries, monitoring lifestyle habits, and providing personalized wellness recovery paths.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* [cite_start]**📊 Smart Mood Check-In**: Uses Natural Language Processing (TextBlob) to analyze the sentiment of your journal entries[cite: 1].
* [cite_start]**🌿 Nature-Centric Scoring**: Calculates a "Wellness Score" based on hours of sleep, screen time, and time spent outdoors[cite: 1].
* [cite_start]**⏰ Personalized Daily Routines**: Generates dynamic schedules (Morning Sunlight, Digital Detox, etc.) based on your calculated burnout risk[cite: 1].
* [cite_start]**🍎 Tailored Nutrition**: Specific meal suggestions based on gender and activity levels[cite: 1].
* [cite_start]**🧘 Mindfulness Suite**: Integrated stress assessments and guided breathing exercises (Box Breathing, Body Scans)[cite: 1].
* [cite_start]**📈 Progress Visualization**: Interactive charts using Altair to track sleep patterns and habit streaks[cite: 1].
